About InnovRise

InnovRise is a Swedish innovation studio based in Karlstad, building ethical, human‑centered technologies that solve real problems in safety, design, and mobility.

Vision

To merge artificial intelligence with human values — creating tools that make people safer, more creative, and more capable.

Principles

  • Ethics by design
  • Privacy‑first architecture
  • Rigorous real‑world testing
  • Accessibility and inclusion

Where We Are

Headquartered in Karlstad, Sweden. We collaborate globally with partners and clients across Europe and beyond.
