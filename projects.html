Projects

A curated portfolio of current and upcoming innovations. We focus on problems with clear human impact — and solve them with elegant engineering.

Project 001 — SafeSpeed BlackBox

Problem: Road accidents, overspeeding, and fatigue are responsible for thousands of avoidable incidents. Insurers lack trusted, objective evidence.

Solution: An AI‑enabled black‑box device for any car that detects overspeeding and driver drowsiness, records encrypted telemetry, and securely shares event summaries with insurance partners.

  • Safety
  • Telematics
  • InsurTech

Project 002 — ArchiPrint AI 3D

Problem: Architectural models are time‑consuming, expensive, and difficult to iterate quickly.

Solution: An AI‑assisted 3D printing workflow that turns sketches and early CAD concepts into optimized printed models — accelerating reviews and reducing cost.

  • DesignTech
  • 3D Printing
  • Architecture

Project 003 — NeuroWake Driver Guard

Problem: Drowsy driving silently causes millions of risky micro‑events before an accident happens.

Solution: A neuro‑audio wearable with ear‑based sensing that detects pre‑sleep patterns and delivers gentle stimuli to keep drivers alert — before danger escalates.

  • NeuroTech
  • Wearables
  • Safety

Project 004 — Jaffar♾️ AI Robot

Problem: Inspection and exploration in complex environments (industrial sites, tight spaces, vertical structures) is dangerous and costly.

Solution: A compact hybrid robot capable of flight and wall‑climbing, with AI vision for mapping, detection, and remote operations.

  • Robotics
  • AI Vision
  • Inspection

Project 005 — BlackBox+ Cloud Hub

Problem: Cities and insurers lack real‑world data to improve safety and policy.

Solution: A privacy‑preserving cloud for aggregated road statistics from InnovRise devices — enabling smarter planning and fairer insurance.

  • Cloud
  • Privacy
  • Analytics
