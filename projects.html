Project 001 — SafeSpeed BlackBox

Problem: Overspeeding and fatigue drive avoidable incidents while insurers lack trusted, objective evidence.

Solution: A plug‑and‑drive AI black‑box that detects overspeed & drowsiness and emits encrypted event summaries — not raw video — to protect privacy while proving the claim.

  • Why now Cheaper sensors + mature edge AI enable mass deployment.
  • Buyer Fleets & insurers who pay for reduced loss ratio.
  • Moat On‑device analytics + cryptographic attestation workflow.

Project 003 — NeuroWake Driver Guard

Problem: Drowsy driving causes micro‑events before disaster.

  • Partner fit Fleets, logistics, and professional drivers.
  • Regulatory Designed for CE marking; data‑minimal by design.
  • Moat Signal processing + adaptive stimulation models.
